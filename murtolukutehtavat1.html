Murtolukutehtävät

Ohje: Kirjoita murtoluku muodossa esim. kaksi kolmasosaa on 2/3. Ilmoita vastaukset supistettuna.

1. \( \frac{3}{8} + \frac{2}{8} = \)

Ratkaisu: \( \frac{3}{8} + \frac{2}{8} = \frac{5}{8} \)

2. \( \frac{7}{10} - \frac{4}{10} = \)

Ratkaisu: \( \frac{7}{10} - \frac{4}{10} = \frac{3}{10} \)

3. \( \frac{5}{6} + \frac{1}{6} = \)

Ratkaisu: \( \frac{5}{6} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{6}{6} = 1 \)

4. \( \frac{9}{12} - \frac{3}{12} = \)

Ratkaisu: \( \frac{9}{12} - \frac{3}{12} = \frac{6}{12} = \frac{1}{2} \)

5. \( \frac{2}{5} + \frac{1}{5} = \)

Ratkaisu: \( \frac{2}{5} + \frac{1}{5} = \frac{3}{5} \)

6. \( \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{6} = \)

Ratkaisu: \( \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{3}{12} + \frac{2}{12} = \frac{5}{12} \)

7. \( \frac{3}{5} - \frac{1}{2} = \)

Ratkaisu: \( \frac{3}{5} - \frac{1}{2} = \frac{6}{10} - \frac{5}{10} = \frac{1}{10} \)

8. \( \frac{2}{3} + \frac{3}{4} = \)

Ratkaisu: \( \frac{2}{3} + \frac{3}{4} = \frac{8}{12} + \frac{9}{12} = \frac{17}{12} = 1\frac{5}{12} \)

9. \( \frac{5}{8} - \frac{1}{3} = \)

Ratkaisu: \( \frac{5}{8} - \frac{1}{3} = \frac{15}{24} - \frac{8}{24} = \frac{7}{24} \)

10. \( \frac{7}{10} + \frac{2}{3} = \)

Ratkaisu: \( \frac{7}{10} + \frac{2}{3} = \frac{21}{30} + \frac{20}{30} = \frac{41}{30} = 1\frac{11}{30} \)
