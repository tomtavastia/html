Murtolukulaskut

1. \( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3} = \)
Ratkaisu: \( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3} = \frac{3}{6} + \frac{2}{6} = \frac{5}{6} \)
2. \( \frac{3}{4} - \frac{1}{2} = \)
Ratkaisu: \( \frac{3}{4} - \frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{4} - \frac{2}{4} = \frac{1}{4} \)
3. \( \frac{2}{5} + \frac{1}{5} = \)
Ratkaisu: \( \frac{2}{5} + \frac{1}{5} = \frac{3}{5} \)
4. \( \frac{5}{6} - \frac{1}{3} = \)
Ratkaisu: \( \frac{5}{6} - \frac{1}{3} = \frac{5}{6} - \frac{2}{6} = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2} \)
5. \( \frac{3}{8} + \frac{1}{4} = \)
Ratkaisu: \( \frac{3}{8} + \frac{1}{4} = \frac{3}{8} + \frac{2}{8} = \frac{5}{8} \)
6. \( \frac{2}{3} - \frac{1}{6} = \)
Ratkaisu: \( \frac{2}{3} - \frac{1}{6} = \frac{4}{6} - \frac{1}{6} = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2} \)
7. \( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{4} = \)
Ratkaisu: \( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{4} = \frac{2}{4} + \frac{1}{4} = \frac{3}{4} \)
8. \( \frac{3}{5} - \frac{2}{5} = \)
Ratkaisu: \( \frac{3}{5} - \frac{2}{5} = \frac{1}{5} \)
9. \( \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{6} = \)
Ratkaisu: \( \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{2}{6} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2} \)
10. \( \frac{4}{7} - \frac{2}{7} = \)
Ratkaisu: \( \frac{4}{7} - \frac{2}{7} = \frac{2}{7} \)
